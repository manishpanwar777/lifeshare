Donation Process

  1. Registration and health history questionnaire
  2. Mini-physical (temperature, blood pressure, pulse, hemoglobin)
  3. The actual blood donation (typically takes 8-10 minutes)
  4. Refreshments and rest (15 minutes)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be at least 17 years old (16 with parental consent in some states)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • Be in good health and feeling well
  • Have not donated whole blood in the last 56 days

Note:

Some medical conditions or medications may affect your eligibility to donate blood. Please consult with our staff if you have any questions or concerns.
