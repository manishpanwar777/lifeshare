About LifeShare

Connecting donors with those in need since 2023

Who We Are

LifeShare is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving lives through blood donation. We connect generous donors with patients in need, ensuring a stable and safe blood supply for our community. Our state-of-the-art facilities and committed team of professionals work tirelessly to make the donation process simple, efficient, and rewarding.

Our Mission

To save lives and improve health through the provision of safe, high-quality blood products and innovative cell therapies, while providing an exceptional experience for our donors.

Our Vision

To be the leading blood donation organization, recognized for excellence in service, innovation, and community impact. We envision a world where no one dies due to a lack of blood.

Our History

2023 - LifeShare is Born

Founded by a group of healthcare professionals and community leaders passionate about addressing the critical need for blood donations.

2024 - Expansion of Services

Launched our mobile blood donation units, bringing donation opportunities directly to communities and workplaces.

2025 - Technological Innovation

Introduced our state-of-the-art online platform, making it easier than ever for donors to schedule appointments and for patients to find blood.
