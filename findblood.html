Blood Request Process

How to Request Blood
  1. Search for available blood units using the form above.
  2. If units are available, contact the listed blood bank or hospital directly.
  3. Provide necessary patient information and medical documentation.
  4. Arrange for transportation of the blood units, if required.
  5. Follow up with the blood bank or hospital for any additional requirements.
Note: In emergency situations, please contact your nearest hospital or our emergency hotline directly.
