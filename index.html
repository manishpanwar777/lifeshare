Why Donate?

Every 2 seconds, someone in the world needs blood. Your donation can save up to 3 lives.

Who Can Donate?

Most healthy adults aged 17 and over can donate blood. Check your eligibility and donate today.

How Often?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days. Platelets can be donated as often as every 7 days.
